## Added from Market Research — June 2026

These came from real user complaints against Tally, QuickBooks, Zoho Books, and Xero.
They are NOT in the MVP but belong in v1.1 or v2.

### High priority (add soon after MVP)
- [ ] Aging receivables report — 30 / 60 / 90+ days buckets  ← should be Basic, not Pro
- [ ] "Send Statement" button per customer → PDF generated + WhatsApp share
- [ ] Credit limit warning at point of sale — red alert if sale would exceed limit
- [ ] One-click full data export to Excel — all customers, transactions, stock
- [ ] Void transaction must require a reason + owner gets notified immediately
- [ ] Post-dated cheque tracking — record cheque date, flag when due (Gulf-specific)

### Medium priority (v2)
- [ ] Dead stock / slow-moving stock report — items with no movement in 60 days
- [ ] Login history visible to owner — who logged in, when, from where
- [ ] Idle session timeout — 30 min warning, auto logout (security)
- [ ] Concurrent session limit — Basic: 2 active sessions, Pro: 10

### Competitive insight
Biggest gap vs competitors: WhatsApp integration + mobile-first + owner language.
Tally scores 9/10 on features but 2/10 on mobile and 3/10 on ease for non-accountants.
TradeFlow wins on mobile + WhatsApp fit (9/10) — protect this advantage.
